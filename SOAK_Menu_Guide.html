Menu Training Reference

This is your menu reference. Return to it before every shift, especially when the menu changes. You are not expected to memorise everything — but you should know every dish well enough to describe it confidently in 2 to 3 lines and make a genuine recommendation when a guest asks.

VEG / NON-VEG RULE: Non-vegetarian items are marked with a red flag on the menu. This is a guide, not a guarantee. If there is any doubt at the pickup counter or at the table, take the dish back to the kitchen and confirm with the counter chef. No guessing. No exceptions.

ALLERGEN NOTE: The allergen information in this document is compiled in good faith. SOAK does not currently operate a fully certified allergen tracking system. If a guest has a serious allergy, flag it on the POS ticket and inform the kitchen and the MOD. Never give a guest false certainty about allergen safety.
